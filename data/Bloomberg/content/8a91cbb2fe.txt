Billionaire Vincent Bolloré has never shied from a fight. But at the influential French newspaper he’s in the process of taking over, the battle lines are hardening.

The businessman dubbed the “French Murdoch” and known as an uncompromising corporate raider has sparked outrage over the naming of a far-right editor-in-chief at Sunday paper Le Journal du Dimanche, prompting a walkout by the newsroom staff that’s in its third week. The move has elicited much hand-wringing by France’s ruling elite over the future direction of the 75-year-old publication that has long played a part in setting the national agenda.
